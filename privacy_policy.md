## 中文版

### 概述

VidBoost 是一款浏览器扩展程序，旨在增强您在网页上观看视频的体验。我们非常重视您的隐私，本隐私政策将解释我们如何处理（或不处理）您的数据。

### 数据收集

**VidBoost 不收集任何用户数据。**

具体来说：
- ❌ 我们不收集个人身份信息
- ❌ 我们不收集浏览历史记录
- ❌ 我们不收集视频观看习惯
- ❌ 我们不追踪用户行为
- ❌ 我们不使用任何分析工具
- ❌ 我们不向第三方发送任何数据

### 本地存储

VidBoost 仅在您的浏览器本地存储以下设置数据：
- 功能开关状态（如是否启用自动暂停）
- 用户偏好设置

这些数据完全存储在您的设备上，绝不会上传到任何服务器。

### 权限说明

VidBoost 请求的浏览器权限及其用途：
- **activeTab**: 用于检测当前标签页中的视频元素
- **storage**: 用于保存您的设置偏好

### 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过以下方式联系我们：
- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/tunecc/VidBoost/issues

## English Version

### Overview

VidBoost is a browser extension designed to enhance your video watching experience on the web. We take your privacy very seriously, and this privacy policy explains how we handle (or don't handle) your data.

### Data Collection

**VidBoost does NOT collect any user data.**

Specifically:
- ❌ We do not collect personal identification information
- ❌ We do not collect browsing history
- ❌ We do not collect video watching habits
- ❌ We do not track user behavior
- ❌ We do not use any analytics tools
- ❌ We do not send any data to third parties

### Local Storage

VidBoost only stores the following settings data locally in your browser:
- Feature toggle states (e.g., whether auto-pause is enabled)
- User preference settings

This data is stored entirely on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

### Permissions Explanation

Browser permissions requested by VidBoost and their purposes:
- **activeTab**: Used to detect video elements in the current tab
- **storage**: Used to save your preference settings

### Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through:
- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/tunecc/VidBoost/issues
